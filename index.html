Experience

Zachary Hayworth has widespread experience as a consultant, advisor, representative, business manager, and entrepreneur. A native of northern New Jersey, he is based in nearby New York City, but has also worked out of cities across the United States to meet the specialized needs of his clients.

As a teenager, Zachary wanted to be able to afford tickets to New Jersey Nets basketball games, so he applied for various retail jobs. When no one would offer him employment, he attempted sell his own sports cards and related collectibles on the internet. He quickly failed, as he found many consumers were only in search of sports collectibles that were extremely old and rare. Soon after, Zachary came up with a concept to identify the oldest possible item on earth and sell it by utilizing the newest e-commerce technologies and internet platforms. As a result, he created a venture that sold prehistoric fossils, uploading the first product listings from his high school library during his junior year. Over time, the company eventually grew to become a leading wholesaler of fossilized commodities, making high volume shipments worldwide on a daily basis. Zachary's experience also includes representating the world's largest producer of prehistoric fossil jewelry. He has negotiated and secured contracts with dozens of businesses on their behalf, including some of the most well-known entertainment centers, luxury resorts, theme parks, museums, aquariums, and key tourist attractions.

Following his years as a business owner, Zachary launched his consulting service to assist other individuals and businesses carve out their own niche, recognize new opportunities, and consistently improve. Additionally, Zachary is a minority owner of, and advisor to, BKB, Inc., the independent export division of India-based Haileyburia Tea Estates Ltd. He serves as the sole company representative in the North American market. Spread over 3,000 acres, the company employs over 1,000 people and produces nearly 4 million pounds (1.8 million kilograms) of black tea annually.

Zachary also has served as a long-time assistant to the Tom Coughlin Jay Fund Foundation, an organization overseen by former National Football League coach and current executive, Thomas R. Coughlin.

Education & Research

Zachary Hayworth received his Bachelor of Science degrees in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He obtained a Master of Business Administration with a management concentration from the University of Massachusetts, which is accompanied by high distinction and a 3.97 grade point average. Following his M.B.A. program, Zachary conducted research at Columbia University in New York City. Through his academic programs, as well as independently, Zachary has researched specific issues within the industries of sports, entertainment, and information science.

Currently, Zachary is a master's candidate at Duke University, where he pursues a specialized academic track in sports business, sports law, and advanced business strategy.
