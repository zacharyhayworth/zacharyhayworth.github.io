Experience

Zachary Hayworth formed The Hayworth Group after a longtime career as an entrepreneur. Unable to find a job while a high school student, Zachary attempted to buy and sell sports collectibles on the internet. While doing so, he realized consumers always sought the most rare and special items. This led to the concept of identifying the world's oldest product and making it readily available through the newest e-commerce platforms at the time. After buying a small amount of prehistoric fossils and creating several product listings, the marketed inventory sold out within hours. Zachary began to negotiate and secure steep price discounts on excess, subpar grade fossils in high volume. The operation eventually evolved to become the leading wholesale distributor of fossilized commodities.

Following his exit from ownership, Zachary launched The Hayworth Group to guide both individuals and businesses on leveraging their strengths and identifying new opportunities. He also maintains holdings in ventures operating within specialty commodities, e-commerce, communications, and sports management.

Education & Research

Zachary Hayworth has studied and conducted research at five different universities. He received his Bachelor of Science degree in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He later obtained a Master of Business Administration with a concentration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with highest distinction.

Additionally, Zachary holds a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. Dr. Paul Haagen of Duke Law School supervised his thesis and research.
