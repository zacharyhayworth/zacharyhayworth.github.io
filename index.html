Our clients have included professionals in private practice, clinic owners, researchers, faculty members, public health directors, specialists, and more. Our areas of expertise include:

About Us

Hayworth Application Services has been serving applicants since 2007. Founded by Zachary Hayworth, who is widely regarded as one of the country’s top admissions consultants, Hayworth Application Services has helped hundreds of international dental and medical applicants gain acceptance to United States programs over the last 15 years. With an extensive network of contacts at the major universities across the country, we are constantly up to date about the content advanced standing and professional programs do and do not want to see in an application. In addition to the impactful writing work and expert advisory we provide, the consistent discussions we have with university personnel has resulted in countless applicants securing acceptance. Hayworth Application Services serves as a private advisor for a very small number of applicants each application cycle who want to maximize the impact their application has on admission committees.

Each application component we prepare or advise on begins with a comprehensive review of a client's candidacy. After the initial assessment, we then plan, organize, and implement a proper strategic presentation of each application component. The ability to be strategic is something many applicants fail to do properly, or even do not do at all. We especially help applicants by using proper psychology and strategy in their writing, leading the university reviewer to envision the applicant as a current professional, rather than an aspiring professional.
