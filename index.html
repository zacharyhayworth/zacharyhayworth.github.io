Experience

Zachary Hayworth formed Hayworth & Co. after a longtime career as an entrepreneur. While a high school student, Zachary attempted to buy and sell sports collectibles when he was unable to find a summer job. During the process, he realized consumers always sought the most outdated items. Zachary subsequently came up with the concept to identify the world's oldest product and make it readily available through the newest internet and e-commerce platforms. After buying a sample of prehistoric fossils and creating several e-commerce product listings, the supply sold out within hours. In the years that followed, Zachary began to secure steep price discounts on excess, subpar grade fossils in high volume through trade shows, private companies, and other channels. The operation eventually evolved to become one of the leading wholesale suppliers of fossilized commodities.

Following his exit from ownership, Zachary launched Hayworth & Co., a consulting and advisory service to guide both individuals and businesses on leveraging their strengths and identifying new opportunities. He also maintains ownership within ventures across specialty commodities, e-commerce, and sports management.

Education & Research

Zachary Hayworth received his Bachelor of Science degree in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He later obtained a Master of Business Administration with a concentration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with highest distinction.

Additionally, Zachary holds a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. His thesis and research advisor was Dr. Paul Haagen of Duke Law School.
