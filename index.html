Application Preparation

Hayworth Application Services provides expert advisory to international professionals applying for advanced programs in dentistry and realted healthcare disciplines.   • Professional writing and editing

  • Strategic CV writing
  • Unparalleled letters of recommendation
  • School-specific essays

About Us

Hayworth Application Services was launched by Zachary Hayworth after a longtime career on at the university level. Hayworth Application Services serves as a private advisor for a very small number of applicants each application cycle who want to maxmize the impact their application has on admissions. Each and every aspect of an applicant's candidacy will be properly organized, prioritized, and presented. With an extensive network of contacts at the major universities across the country, we are constantly up to date about the content these programs do and do not want to see in an application. In addition to the impactful writing work and expert advisory we provide, the consistent discussions we have with university personnel are why our service has been able to successfully help so many applicants secure acceptance.

Following his exit from ownership, Zachary founded The Hayworth Group to guide individuals and organizations on leveraging strengths and identifying new opportunities. He continues to maintain ownership holdings in ventures operating within specialty commodities, e-commerce, and sports.

Education & Research

Our founder, Zachary Hayworth has studied and conducted research at six different universities. The workspace of Hayworth Application Services is in New Jersey, near Rutgers University. We have been helping serving applicants for 15 years, since 2007. Over this period of time we have helped hundreds of international dental applicants gain acceptance to United States programs.
